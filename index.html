What is Hac'K_23 ?

HAC'K 23 is 24hrs software based hackathon organizing by CS Dept of KMEA in connection with tech fest-Kapricious'23.

Who can participate?

Hac'K_23 is open for students all over India .

What's the team size?

Eack team can contain 2-3 members.

How can I register for Hac'K_23 hackathon?

The registration would be simply done by just clicking the register button and filling up a form in which you would have to mention you and your team details. Registrations end on 25th April 2023.

Is there any registration fee for participating in Hac'k_23 ?

No. The Hackathon is free for all participants

What kind of projects can I work on at Hac'k_23?

You can work at any kind of projects based on BlockChain , AgroTech and MediTech.

What if I don't know coding ?

Hac'K_23 is a software based Hackathon . Atleast you need the basic knowledge of Coding.
